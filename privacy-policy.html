Privacy Policy

Click Start Trucking LLC — How we collect, use, and protect your information

Click Start Trucking LLC 1700 Northside Dr NW, Suite A7 PMB 2019, Atlanta, GA 30318

Effective Date: May 24, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Click Start Trucking LLC collects the following categories of personal information when you submit our driver application form or otherwise interact with our services:

  • Name: First and last name for identification and application processing.
  • Phone Number: Your mobile or landline phone number, used to contact you about your driver application and, if you opt in, to send SMS messages.
  • Email Address: Used for application correspondence and notifications.
  • CDL (Commercial Driver License) Information: CDL class (A, B, or C) to assess driver qualifications.
  • SMS Consent Status: Whether you have opted in or opted out of receiving SMS messages from us.

We collect this information directly from you when you voluntarily submit our online driver application form. We do not collect information from purchased, rented, or third-party affiliate lead lists.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Driver Recruiting: To evaluate your application and determine your suitability for driving positions with Click Start Trucking LLC.
  • Onboarding: To guide you through the onboarding process, including document collection and orientation scheduling.
  • Compliance Reminders: To send reminders about required documentation, certifications, and compliance deadlines.
  • Application Status Updates: To notify you of changes to your application status.
  • SMS Communications: If you have opted in, to send text messages regarding your application, onboarding steps, document requests, and status updates.

3. SMS Communications

By opting in to SMS communications, you consent to receive automated SMS messages from Click Start Trucking LLC at the phone number you provided.

  • Message & data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
  • Message frequency varies based on your application status and onboarding stage.
  • You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive a one-time confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent.
  • For assistance, reply HELP to any SMS message, or contact us at admin@clickstarttruck.com or (470) 670-0970.
  • Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of any purchase or service.

SMS opt-in information is collected solely through our opt-in form at clickstarttruck.com/opt-in.html. We do not obtain SMS opt-in consent from any third-party sources, purchased lists, or affiliate databases.

4. Information Sharing

Click Start Trucking LLC is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, rent, lease, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Your phone number and SMS consent information are NEVER shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Phone numbers collected for SMS communications will not be shared with any other company, organization, or individual for marketing or promotional use.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business (e.g., SMS messaging platforms), who are contractually bound to keep your information confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets, with appropriate confidentiality protections.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Specifically:

  • Driver application data is retained for up to 3 years following the conclusion of the recruitment process, or as required by applicable employment and trucking regulations.
  • SMS opt-in/opt-out records are retained indefinitely to maintain accurate consent records and comply with carrier and regulatory requirements.
  • If you request deletion of your personal data, we will make commercially reasonable efforts to comply, except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Opt-Out of SMS: Reply STOP to any SMS message to immediately unsubscribe. You can also contact us at admin@clickstarttruck.com to be removed from our SMS list.
  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal obligations.
  • Complaints: If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the applicable regulatory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in the Contact Us section below.

7. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the Effective Date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
